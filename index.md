## This website is still being synthesized!

 This GitHub Pages site is designed to serve as a front end for software and hardware projects developed by the Greytak Chemistry Laboratory at the University of South Carolina. We make much use of Matlab tools for spectroscopy, particle image analysis, and isothermal titration calorimetry (ITC), and we are also interested in hardware development including Arduino-based instrumentation. Our official group website with info on group members, publications, and educational efforts is linked at left.
