# Public projects

Here are some projects you can check out now: click the Github link at left to go to them directly. If you've heard us talking about something cool and it's not here, it's probably not accessible yet.

- matlab-fluorescent-particle-image-analysis: a script that can load, label, and analyze properties of particles in a series of images, with various options for cropping and region of interest specifications. 

- matlab-chemists-toolbox: a tutorial for plotting data, matrix math, curve fitting, image analysis and programming in Matlab, with examples from physical chemistry. An opportunity to practice your Matlab or your p-chem.
